My Favorite Animals

Cat

Cats are small, carnivorous mammals that are often kept as pets. They are known for their agility and playful behavior.

Dog

Dogs are loyal and friendly animals. They are considered man's best friend and come in many breeds.

Rabbit

Rabbits are small mammals with fluffy tails and long ears. They are herbivorous and love carrots.
