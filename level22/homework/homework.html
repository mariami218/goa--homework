My Favourite Foods

Pizza

Pizza is a delicious Italian dish made of a round, flat base of dough topped with cheese, tomatoes, and various other ingredients. It's crispy, cheesy, and full of flavor!

Sushi

Sushi is a Japanese dish consisting of rice, seaweed, and raw fish. It is often served with wasabi and soy sauce, and it offers a unique blend of flavors.

Ice Cream

Ice cream is a frozen dessert made from milk, cream, sugar, and flavorings. It's sweet, creamy, and comes in many flavors like chocolate, vanilla, and strawberry.
