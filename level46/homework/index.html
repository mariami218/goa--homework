Simple Omelette Recipe

A classic and easy-to-make omelette for your breakfast.

Preparation Time

  • Prep: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp butter or oil
  • Optional: Cheese, herbs, veggies

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs in a bowl with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat butter or oil in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour the eggs into the pan and cook until slightly set.
  4. Fold the omelette and serve warm.
